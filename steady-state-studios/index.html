About

Built by one person. Made to last.

Steady State Studios is an independent software studio based in Melbourne. It's small by design — one developer, a short list of products, and a preference for getting things right over getting things out fast.

How we work

A few things we don't compromise on.

Privacy

Your data is yours

Free tiers keep data on-device wherever possible. Paid sync is opt-in, isolated per account, and never sold or shared with advertisers.

Honesty

No dark patterns

Clear pricing, clear cancellation, no manufactured urgency. If a recommendation earns us a commission, we say so — and we don't let it change the advice.

Craft

Maintained, not abandoned

Small studio means a short list of products — and real ongoing attention to each one, rather than a graveyard of shipped-and-forgotten apps.
